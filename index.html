Welcome to the Home of Authentic West African Cuisine.

Menu

“Strangers, friends or family, the way to happiness is through the stomach.”

Mama Ashanti’s food is made with the freshest ingredients and bold spices, following original recipes passed down from generation to generation. Every dish is an expression of our rich West African culture, made with love, from our mother’s pot to your plate. Dig in and don’t shy away from asking for seconds.
